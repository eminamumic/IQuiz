Welcome to IQuiz!

IQuiz is a quiz game designed to challenge your intellect with a variety of categories. You can test your knowledge on topics like Science, History, Literature, and more!

How to Play:

To start playing:

  1. Select a category (you can only choose one).
  2. Pick your preferred difficulty level (Easy, Medium, Hard).
  3. Decide how many questions you'd like to answer.
  4. Then, just hit start and test your brainpower!

Ready to challenge yourself? Good luck!
